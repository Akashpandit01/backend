# Prompt

Explain step-by-step how to renovate a kitchen in a modern style under a limited budget.

Include:
- Planning process
- Cost estimation
- Affordable material suggestions
- Final design ideas

# AI Response

Step 1 – Set Budget
Decide total renovation budget.

Step 2 – Identify Key Areas
Focus on cabinets, lighting, countertops.

Step 3 – Affordable Materials
Use laminate cabinets instead of solid wood.

Step 4 – Lighting
Install LED strip lighting for modern look.

Step 5 – Appliances
Buy energy-efficient but budget appliances.

Step 6 – Final Layout
Open design with minimal clutter.
